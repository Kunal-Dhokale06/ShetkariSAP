## PROJECT

### 🌾 किसान आर्थिक सहाय्यक (Kisan Finance Assistant)

Build a mobile application that helps Indian farmers manage their farming finances and agricultural activities in one place.

The application should allow farmers to:

* Track multiple farms and plots
* Manage crop-wise investments
* Record expenses
* Record crop sales
* Calculate profit and loss
* Maintain crop diaries
* Generate reports
* Plan budgets
* Receive AI-powered financial insights
* Use voice input
* Scan bills automatically
* Work completely offline
* Synchronize automatically when internet becomes available

The application should be extremely simple because many users will be first-time smartphone users. Every screen should be designed specifically for rural farmers with large touch targets, minimal steps, clean layouts, and excellent readability under sunlight.
